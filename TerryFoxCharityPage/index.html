About Us

Why Choose Us

Follow In Terry's Footsteps To Conquer Cancer

In 1980, Terry Fox embarked on a Marathon of Hope, a courageous cross-Canada run on one leg, to raise awareness and funds for cancer research, epitomizing the indomitable spirit of resilience and hope. Inspired by Terry's extraordinary journey, the Terry Fox Foundation was established in his honor, continuing his mission by uniting communities across the globe to support cancer research and embody the belief that one person's dream can indeed change the world.
